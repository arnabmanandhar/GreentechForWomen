About Us

Greentech For Women (GFW) is a non-profit, non-political, and non-partisan organization based in Nepal.

"Climate justice is not possible without gender justice."

Founded

Established as a legally registered non-profit organization in Nepal

Mission

Advancing ecofeminist solutions at the intersection of gender and climate justice

Impact

Representing grassroots voices on global platforms and policy forums
